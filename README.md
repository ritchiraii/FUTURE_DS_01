# Project Overview

Revenue reached **$2.30M**, yet **average order value declined**.

To understand what was driving business performance, this project performs an **end-to-end Retail Sales Analysis**, building an interactive dashboard using **Microsoft Power BI** to transform raw transactional data into **clear, decision-ready insights**.

The objective was not only to visualize sales data but to **identify patterns influencing revenue, profitability, product performance, and discount strategy**.

---

# Key Insights from the Analysis

- Revenue growth is primarily driven by **higher order volume**
- **Average order value is declining**, suggesting opportunities for **pricing optimization or product bundling**
- The **Technology category delivers the strongest profit margins**
- **Furniture shows weaker profitability due to higher discount levels**

To surface these findings quickly, the dashboard includes a **Smart Business Insights Panel**, powered by calculated measures that automatically highlights important patterns in the data.

---

# Dashboard Features

- Executive **KPI Cards** displaying Revenue, Orders, Profit, Profit Margin, and Discount Rate
- **Interactive Filters** for Month, Year, and Scope
- **Custom Visual Tooltips** providing deeper analytical context
- **Drill-down capability** across region, category, and product levels
- **Product-level profitability table** with margin health indicators

---

# Business Analysis Included

- Identification of **top revenue-generating products**
- **Discount vs Profitability analysis**
- **Customer segment revenue distribution**
- **Geographic revenue and margin performance**
- **Product-level margin analysis**

These analyses help highlight **high-performing product categories and areas where discount strategies impact profitability**.
